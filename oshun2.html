She is the third and favorite wife of Shango and the mother of the ibeji, the sacred twins. Though she is known as one of the most powerful Orishas and is usually benevolent, Oshun still demonstrates human emotions like spite and envy. In a fit of jealousy, she convinced Shango’s first wife, Oba, to cut off her ear so that she would no longer rival Oshun’s beauty. However, even with these tendencies, she is often seen as a protector, nurture, and one who gives life.
